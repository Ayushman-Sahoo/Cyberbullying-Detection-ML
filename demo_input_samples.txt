Project 8 - Cyberbullying Detection Demo Inputs
===============================================

Usage
-----
Enter one full message at a time in the frontend text box.


Sample 1 - Clean / supportive
-----------------------------
I appreciate how you handled this discussion. Thank you for being respectful.

Sample 2 - Clean / neutral
--------------------------
Please submit your assignment before 5 PM and review the comments from your teacher.

Sample 3 - Mildly toxic
-----------------------
Your idea is dumb and you never understand anything.

Sample 4 - Explicit bullying
----------------------------
You are worthless and nobody wants to talk to you.

Sample 5 - Threatening tone
---------------------------
If you show up again, I will make sure you regret it.

Sample 6 - Mixed sentence
-------------------------
You did one thing right today, but most of your work is still pathetic.
